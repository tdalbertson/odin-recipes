Pork Katsu

Description

One of Japan's specialties is Tonkatsu (とんかつ、豚カツ) or deep-fried pork cutlet.

Although it may seem challenging to make this Japanese staple, it is actually quite easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour
  • Panko
  • Neutral flavored oil for deep frying
  • Tonkatsu sauce
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Shredded Cabbage (optional)

Instructions

Preparing the Shredded Cabbage (Optional)

  1. You can cut the cabbage extremely thinly with a sharp knife or use a cabbage slicer.
